Vera | From source documents to a working client file

This Core edition shows how to build a new client’s file from documents and other available source evidence. The first step records the incoming documents and where they came from. The second connects client identity with the people involved. The third organizes requested services and engagement facts. Open requests, actions, dates and history remain distinct but connected, so every fact can be traced to its source or the decision behind it. Each country maps its own documents, checks and rules onto this structure, producing a readable client file that shows what is known, what remains open and how the professional relationship develops.
