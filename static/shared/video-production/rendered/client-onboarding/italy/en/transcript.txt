Vera | Client onboarding · Italy

A new client brings documents, data, people and decisions that often remain in separate files. Vera carries them through one connected journey. It starts with the incoming folder, builds the inventory, gathers readable data and turns open points into precise requests. It then uses that same evidence to structure identity data, executors, beneficial owners, services and engagement terms. Engagement, privacy, AI information and the AML assessment share one basis. Actions, dates and reviews complete the file. The firm can see what is ready, what needs completing and which step comes next. From the first folder to an operational file that continues to follow the client.
