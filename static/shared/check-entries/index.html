Inputs

What it reads

The preferred source is structured FatturaPA XML. Connector exports remain read-only and authorized; PDFs are the final targeted fallback.

Outputs

What it produces

The result records XML and PDF inventories, connector provenance, row-level matches, exceptions and the complete review trail.

    Entry checks tied to documents

    The inspection step proposes movement number, date, amount, debit/credit and beneficiary fields, then asks only for unresolved choices.

    A FatturaPA invoice is accepted automatically only when one candidate matches at least two independent fields.

    Multiple XML candidates, unreadable files and weak matches remain exceptions; Vera asks for a PDF only when that targeted evidence is still needed.

    Use

    Start broad, finish with targeted requests

    Provide the sample and the best available source. Vera narrows the population before asking anyone to retrieve individual documents.
