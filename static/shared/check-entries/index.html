Specifiche tecniche, fonti e file prodotti

Inputs

What it reads

The check starts from sampled rows and the best available source without assuming a national format.

Outputs

What it produces

The work folder records sources, row-level matches, exceptions and the review trail.

    Entry checks tied to documents

    The inspection step proposes movement number, date, amount, debit/credit and beneficiary fields, then asks only for unresolved choices.

    A supporting record is connected automatically only when one candidate is backed by at least two independent fields.

    Multiple candidates, unreadable files and weak matches remain exceptions with the exact source that needs review.

    Use

    Start broad, finish with targeted requests

    Provide the sample and the best available source. Vera narrows the population before asking anyone to retrieve individual documents.
